Well what is coffee?

Coffee is a very old drink that has been consumed for centuries, It can be very bitter if not made correctly but when made right it can be very smooth and lovely tasting.

Coffee is made by roasting coffee beans and then grinding them into a powder.

Once made into a powder you put it ontop of a filter and pour hot water into it slowly so it can drip lovely coffee down into your cup and thats just one way to make it!

Coffee makes the world go round!

Types of Coffee

There are so many types of coffee! From all over the world coffee is made and sold with different flavours and methods of creating coffee from beans.

The main kinds people know.

  • Espresso - A small amount of near boiling water pushed through coffe grounds.
  • Americano - coffee made of 1 part espresso and 3 to 4 parts hot water.
  • Cappuccino - Coffee made with an espresso and steamed milk/cream.
  • Latte - Coffee made the same way as a Cappuccino but with a different taste and cream rating.
  • Macchiato - Coffe made of an espresso and a dash of milk.
  • Mocha - Coffee made with espresso, milk/cream and a tasty portion of chocolate powder/syrup.

Some less known ones.

  • Ristretto - Coffee made similar to an espresso but with less water as to make it stronger.
  • Flat White - Coffe made the same way as a Latte but with a stronger concentration of espresso.
  • Affogato - a nice scoop of vanilla icecream doused in a shot of esspresso.
  • Irish Coffee - Coffee made with Irish whiskey, coffee and sugar.
  • Turkish Coffee - Coffee made unfiltered and in a sepcial tool called a Cezve.. Ooh interesting.
  • Viennese Coffee - Coffee made with an espresso that is combined with melted chocolate and toped with whipped cream.
  • Café au Lait - Coffee made with warm milk, not cold milk.
  • Café de Olla - Coffee made inside a traditional Mexican earthen clay pot as to give it an different flavour.

Thats enough kinds of coffee, my heads starting to spin!
